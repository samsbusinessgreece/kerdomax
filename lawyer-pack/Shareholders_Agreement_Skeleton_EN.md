Section 1 – Parties
This Agreement is made between:
- Sam Erik J Depoortere (50%)
- Artemis Tsami (50%)

Section 2 – Purpose
To govern shareholding, governance, and post-formation arrangements for KERDOMAX I.K.E.

Section 3 – Capital & Shares
- Total capital: €100; 100 shares of €1 each
- Ownership: Sam 50%, Artemis 50%

Section 4 – Management & Signing Authority
- Two Managing Directors with equal rights. For ordinary matters, each MD may sign on behalf of the Company; for Reserved/Major matters, joint consent or a defined threshold is required.

Section 5 – Decision-Making & Deadlock
- In the event of a tie, a 30-day cooling-off period shall apply. If unresolved, mediation, then buy-sell options or a mutually agreed third party decision maker.

Section 6 – Transfer of Shares
- ROFR; pre-emption rights for the other shareholder; transfer to third parties restricted.

Section 7 – Non-Compete & IP
- Non-compete for 2 years post-exit; IP developed remains with the Company.

Section 8 – Transfer of IP & Confidentiality
- IP and confidentiality provisions; ownership of developed IP.

Section 9 – Governing Law & Dispute Resolution
- Greece; mediation then arbitration if needed.

Section 10 – Miscellaneous
- Any other clauses as needed.
