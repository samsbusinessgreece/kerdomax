## Section 1 — Purpose
This Agreement governs the shareholding, management, decision-making, and exit provisions for KERDOMAX I.K.E., a Greek Private Company (IKE) registered at Dasous 2, 15233 Chalandri, Greece.

## Section 2 — Capital & Shares
- Total share capital: €100.00
- 100 equity shares of €1.00 each
- Sam Erik J Depoortere: 51 shares (51%) — €51.00
- Artemis Tsami: 49 shares (49%) — €49.00

## Section 3 — Management & Signing Authority
- Two Managing Directors (Διαχειριστές): Sam Erik J Depoortere and Artemis Tsami
- Both MDs may individually sign on behalf of the Company for ordinary business matters
- Sam holds 51% and therefore the deciding vote in case of disagreement on ordinary matters

## Section 4 — Reserved Matters (Requiring Joint Consent)
The following actions require the written consent of both shareholders, regardless of the 51/49 split:
1. Taking on debt or loans exceeding €5,000 (threshold to be confirmed with lawyer)
2. Changes to the company's business scope or purpose
3. Admission of new shareholders or issuance of new shares
4. Sale, licensing, or transfer of company IP (including the KERDOMAX brand)
5. Related-party transactions
6. Any single expenditure exceeding €10,000 (threshold to be confirmed)
7. Amendments to the Articles of Association
8. Voluntary dissolution of the company

## Section 5 — Decision-Making & Deadlock
- Ordinary decisions: majority vote (Sam's 51% is decisive)
- Reserved matters: unanimous consent required
- In the event of deadlock on a reserved matter:
  - 30-day cooling-off period
  - If unresolved: mandatory mediation by a mutually agreed mediator
  - If still unresolved: buy-sell mechanism (one party offers a price; the other must either buy at that price or sell at that price)

## Section 6 — Transfer of Shares
- Right of First Refusal (ROFR): if either party wishes to sell their shares, they must first offer them to the other party at the same price and on the same terms
- Pre-emption period: 30 days to accept or decline
- No transfer to third parties without the other shareholder's written consent
- Permitted transfers: to immediate family members or a holding entity wholly owned by the transferring shareholder, subject to written notice

## Section 7 — Non-Compete
- Both parties agree not to engage in, own, or operate a competing short-term rental optimization business in Greece for a period of 2 years following their departure from the company
- This restriction applies within the Greek territory and the broader Mediterranean/Southeast European market

## Section 8 — Intellectual Property
- All intellectual property developed by either party in connection with KERDOMAX (including but not limited to code, algorithms, branding, trademarks, customer data, and proprietary methodologies) belongs 100% to KERDOMAX I.K.E.
- Neither party may use, license, or transfer company IP without the written consent of both shareholders

## Section 9 — Confidentiality
- Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business information, customer data, financial details, and proprietary methods of the company
- This obligation survives termination of this Agreement and any departure from the company for a period of 5 years

## Section 10 — Profit Distribution
- Profits shall be distributed in proportion to shareholding (51/49) unless otherwise agreed in writing by both parties
- Distribution timing: annually or as decided by the MDs, subject to legal reserves

## Section 11 — Governing Law & Dispute Resolution
- Governing law: Greek law
- Dispute resolution: mediation in Athens, Greece → if unresolved, binding arbitration under Greek arbitration rules

## Section 12 — Miscellaneous
- This Agreement may be amended only by written consent of both parties
- If any provision is found unenforceable, the remainder of the Agreement shall continue in full force
- This Agreement supersedes any prior oral or written agreements between the parties regarding KERDOMAX I.K.E.
