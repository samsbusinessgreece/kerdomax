# KERDOMAX Visual Identity — Design Brief (Architectural Axis)

## 1. Design Concept

The logo represents defined structural space. The geometry of the custom K monogram is built around a structural void suggesting an architectural atrium, with a visible "V" shape cut through the core. It uses authoritative light and precise geometry to define architectural volume.

## 2. Core Components

### 2a. The 'Axis' Monogram (K Emblem)

- **Geometry:** Unique, non-standard geometric K monogram from precisely aligned polygons. Stem = Slate Blue; right cross-beams = Warm Beige.
- **Void Principle:** Must maintain geometric structural void through the cross-beams. Defined by negative space and light. Negative space is critical.
- **Proportions:** Per the 8x8 Construction Grid. All elements precisely measured; must not be altered.

### 2b. Primary Wordmark

- **Font:** Montserrat Bold (or similar precise geometric sans-serif).
- **Wording:** "KERDOMAX" (uppercase only).
- **Color Logic:** KERDO = Slate Blue; MAX = Warm Beige. Mandatory for full-color use.

### 2c. Primary Horizontal Lockup

- **Assembly:** K monogram left of KERDOMAX wordmark.
- **Alignment:** Precise vertical alignment (monogram center = text center). Clear space = height of wordmark on all sides.

## 3. Color Palette (Non-Negotiable)

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary/Authoritative | Slate Blue | `#1A2E4C` |
| Accent/Conceptual | Warm Beige | `#EDE7DD` |

## 4. Grid & Construction

- **8x8 Grid:** Definitive blueprint for monogram. All angles, stem widths, cross-beam placement, and central void size are fixed.
- **App Icons:** K mark in curved Slate Blue rounded rectangle, cross-beams in Warm Beige. Maintain proportions at 512px and 64x64px.

## 5. Deliverables & Usage

All assets vector-first (AI/EPS/SVG).

### Variants

- **Full Color (Primary):** Slate Blue stem + Warm Beige beams; KERDO Blue + MAX Beige on white/neutral.
- **Full Color on Dark:** Blue and Beige inverted on dark navy/slate blue field.
- **Monochrome Navy:** All elements in `#1A2E4C`.
- **Monochrome Reverse:** All elements in white.

### Core Rule

Stem = always blue and authoritative. Cross-beams = always beige and light. Do not invert in standard lockup.
