# KERDOMAX Market Validation Plan (Greek STR Focus) - March 2026

## Objective
Validate real market demand for KERDOMAX among Greek short-term rental hosts and property managers, quantify willingness to pay, and define a concrete MVP scope informed by real feedback.

## Target Market & Personas
- Primary: Greek hosts with 1–2 properties (individual operators) and small PMs managing 2–10 units.
- Geography: Athens, Thessaloniki, Crete, Rhodes, and popular island destinations with high STR activity.
- Pain points to explore:
  - Pricing optimization and occupancy stability
  - Time spent on listing management across platforms
  - Quality of guest messaging and support
  - Awareness and handling of local Greek regulations

## Validation Methods (2-week plan)
- In-depth Interviews: 12–20 short interviews (15–20 minutes) to uncover pain points, willingness to pay, and MVP feature interest.
- Landing Page Signups: Build a simple landing page to capture interest (target 50–100 signups/leads).
- Quick Online Survey: 5–8 questions deployed via form or DM to gather quick signals.
- Outreach Campaign: Targeted messages in Greek host communities and personal networks to secure interview slots and land signups.

## MVP Scope for Validation
Based on early feedback, validate a compact MVP with:
- Dynamic pricing hub tailored to Greek market signals
- Simple listing optimization prompts (titles/descriptions) per listing
- Basic guest messaging templates with localization
- Core dashboard (revenue, occupancy, ADR indicators)

Exclude advanced automation features until validation confirms demand and ROI.
